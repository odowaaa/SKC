About SomaliKing College

A career-focused, community-responsive college built in 2026 for Somali learners who need practical education, strong communication skills, and employable knowledge.

Our Identity

Somali Identity, Leadership, and Excellence

The name SomaliKing College reflects three ideas: Somali identity, leadership, and excellence. SKC is modern in method, strong in discipline, and respectful of local culture and values — preparing Somali learners for a better future through strong English, professional skills, practical training, discipline, and service to society.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted Somali-centered college for English, technology, engineering, social impact, and health skills.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, practical, high-quality education that builds competent graduates who can work, lead, communicate, and solve local problems.

Curriculum Philosophy

Competency Before Memorization

The SKC curriculum is based on competency-based education and training: learners must demonstrate what they can do, not only what they can remember.

  • Competence before memorization — each course produces an observable skill
  • Somalia-context learning that reflects local business, work, health, and social realities
  • Progressive English development from survival English to academic and professional English
  • Employability and entrepreneurship built into every program
  • Flexible delivery for full-time, evening, weekend, and blended learners

"The book is intentionally practical. It is designed for Somalia's education and labor context, where students often need English, office skills, technical competence, and confidence to work in real environments such as businesses, NGOs, schools, construction sites, clinics, and service offices."

SKC Curriculum Book, Edition 2.0

Graduate Profile

What an SKC Graduate Looks Like

A SKC graduate is confident, disciplined, practical, and ready for further study or employment.

Communicates in English at an appropriate level for study and work.

Uses computers, digital tools, and office applications confidently.

Applies technical and professional knowledge in real tasks.

Writes reports, emails, proposals, and professional documents.

Works safely, ethically, and respectfully with others.

Participates in teamwork, innovation, and community improvement.

Leadership & Governance

A Clear, Accountable Structure

SKC maintains a simple but professional governance structure to keep teaching, records, and operations accountable.

Principal / College Director

Overall leadership and external representation.

Academic Coordinator

Timetable, assessment, quality assurance, and teacher support.

Heads of School

Manage English, IT, Engineering, Social & Human Services, and Health.

Registrar

Student records, admissions, transcripts, and certification.

Finance & Operations Officer

Fees, budgets, procurement, and resource control.

Student Support Officer

Attendance, discipline, guidance, and learner welfare.
