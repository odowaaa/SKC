## SECTION A: LISTENING (20 marks)

*Teacher reads each item twice at normal speed.*

**Listening Script (Teacher's copy):**

> "Good morning. My name is Nasra. I am 29 years old, from Berbera. I have a small family: my husband and two children. I work as a nurse at the community clinic. My house has three rooms. Every day, I wake up at six o'clock, eat breakfast, and go to work by bus. My favorite color is orange, and my favorite food is fish with rice. Today, the weather is cloudy and cool."

**A1. Answer the questions (12 marks):**
1. What is her name and age?
2. Where is she from?
3. Who is in her family?
4. What is her job?
5. How many rooms does her house have?
6. What time does she wake up?

**A2. Circle True or False (8 marks):**
1. Nasra works as a teacher. (True / False)
2. She goes to work by bus. (True / False)
3. Her favorite color is orange. (True / False)
4. The weather today is sunny and hot. (True / False)

## SECTION B: READING (20 marks)

**Read the passage and answer the questions.**

> My name is Yusuf. I am a student at SKC and also work part-time as a shopkeeper. I am 24 years old, from Merca. I live with my parents and one brother in a small house near the market. Every morning, I walk to my shop and open it at eight o'clock. I sell fruit, vegetables, and bread. In the afternoon, I come to SKC for my English class. My teacher is very patient, and I am learning a lot. My goal is to become a businessman one day. On weekends, the weather is usually sunny, and I enjoy spending time with my family.

**B1. Comprehension Questions (12 marks):**
1. What two things does Yusuf do (student and ___)?
2. How old is he, and where is he from?
3. Who does he live with?
4. What does his shop sell?
5. What time does he open his shop?
6. What is his future goal?

**B2. Circle the Correct Answer (8 marks):**
1. Yusuf is from (Merca / Berbera).
2. His shop opens at (seven / eight) o'clock.
3. He lives near the (market / hospital).
4. His goal is to become a (teacher / businessman).

## SECTION C: GRAMMAR (20 marks)

**C1. Fill in the blank with the correct "be" verb (5 marks):**

1. I __________ a student.
2. She __________ a nurse.
3. They __________ my classmates.
4. He __________ from Somalia.
5. We __________ happy today.

**C2. Fill in with "a" or "an" (5 marks):**

1. __________ engineer
2. __________ book
3. __________ umbrella
4. __________ pen
5. __________ orange

**C3. Fill in with "this/that/these/those," "there is/there are," or a preposition of place (5 marks):**

1. __________ is my house. (near)
2. __________ are my books. (near, plural)
3. __________ a bed in my room. (there is/there are)
4. The clinic is __________ the school. (next to)
5. The market is __________ the mosque and the bank. (between)

**C4. Fill in with the correct simple present verb form (5 marks):**

1. I __________ (study) English every day.
2. She __________ (work) as a teacher.
3. They __________ (go) to school by bus.
4. He __________ (live) near the market.
5. We __________ (eat) dinner at seven o'clock.

## SECTION E: SPEAKING (20 marks) — *Assessed individually by teacher*

**Speaking Tasks:**

1. Introduce yourself completely: name, age, country, family. (5 marks)
2. Describe your house and school in three sentences each. (5 marks)
3. Answer three spontaneous questions from the teacher (e.g., "What is your job?" "How is the weather?" "How do you travel to school?"). (5 marks)
4. Give a one-minute mini version of your Final Project presentation. (5 marks)

**Teacher's Speaking Assessment Rubric:**

| Criteria | Excellent (5) | Good (4) | Fair (3) | Needs Practice (2) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Clarity | Very clear pronunciation | Mostly clear | Sometimes unclear | Hard to understand |
| Confidence | Very confident | Confident | A little nervous | Very nervous |
| Vocabulary Range | Uses vocabulary from many units | Uses vocabulary from most units | Uses limited vocabulary | Uses very few words |
| Grammar Accuracy | No errors | Few errors | Some errors | Many errors |
| Fluency | Speaks smoothly | Speaks with minor pauses | Speaks with frequent pauses | Struggles to speak |
