## SECTION A: LISTENING (20 marks)

*Teacher reads each item twice at normal speed.*

**Listening Script (Teacher's copy):**

> "Good morning. My name is Sadia. I am 27 years old. I am from Baidoa. I have two brothers and one sister. My favorite color is blue. I eat breakfast at seven o'clock. Today is Monday, and my English class is at nine o'clock."

**A1. Answer the questions (10 marks):**
1. What is her name? __________
2. How old is she? __________
3. Where is she from? __________
4. How many brothers does she have? __________
5. What is her favorite color? __________

**A2. Circle True or False (10 marks):**
1. She eats breakfast at seven o'clock. (True / False)
2. Today is Tuesday. (True / False)
3. Her class is at nine o'clock. (True / False)
4. She has one brother. (True / False)
5. She is from Hargeisa. (True / False)

## SECTION B: READING (20 marks)

**Read the passage and answer the questions.**

> My name is Jamal. I am a student at SKC. I am 23 years old. I have a small family — my mother, my father, and one sister. Every day, I wake up at six o'clock. I eat breakfast, and then I go to school. My class starts at eight o'clock. I like my English class very much. My favorite color is green, and my favorite food is rice with chicken.

**B1. Comprehension Questions (10 marks):**
1. How old is Jamal?
2. How many people are in his family?
3. What time does he wake up?
4. What time does class start?
5. What is his favorite food?

**B2. Circle the Correct Answer (10 marks):**
1. Jamal is a (teacher / student).
2. His favorite color is (green / blue).
3. He wakes up at (six / seven) o'clock.
4. His family is (big / small).
5. He eats rice with (chicken / fish).

## SECTION C: GRAMMAR (20 marks)

**C1. Fill in the blank with "am," "is," or "are" (10 marks):**

3. They __________ my friends.

## SECTION E: SPEAKING (20 marks) — *Assessed individually by teacher*

**Speaking Tasks:**
1. Introduce yourself (name, age, country). (5 marks)

| Criteria | Excellent (5) | Good (4) | Fair (3) | Needs Practice (2) |
