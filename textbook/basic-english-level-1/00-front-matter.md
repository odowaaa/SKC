## Preface

Welcome to **Basic English Level 1: Survival English**, the first book in the SomaliKing College (SKC) English language series.

This book was created for one reason: to help adult Somali learners speak, read, and write real English from the very first day of class. Many students who begin this course have never studied English before. Some have never held a pen in a classroom. This book was written with that learner in mind — simple language, large pictures, short sentences, and steady repetition.

Every unit in this book connects to real Somali life: greeting a neighbor, introducing your family, buying vegetables at the market, telling a taxi driver where to go, filling out a form at a government office, or answering the phone. These are not abstract grammar exercises. They are survival skills — the English a person needs to work, study, and live with confidence.

The SKC Curriculum Development Team designed this book around four goals:

1. **Simplicity** — Pre-A1 level language that never overwhelms a true beginner.
2. **Relevance** — Somali names, Somali settings, and Somali cultural context in every unit.
3. **Practice** — Every new word and structure is practiced through speaking, listening, reading, and writing.
4. **Confidence** — Small, achievable steps that let students feel success every day.

We are proud to bring this original textbook to SKC classrooms. We hope it helps every student build a foundation for a better future.

**— SKC Curriculum Development Team**

**Tips for Students**
- Speak out loud. Do not be afraid to make mistakes.
- Look at every picture. Pictures help you remember words.
- Practice every day, even for 10 minutes.
- Bring a notebook and pen to every class.
- Ask your teacher when you do not understand. This is normal and good.

**Tips for Teachers**
Teacher-facing tips, lesson plans, and answer keys for every unit are provided in the separate **SKC Teacher's Resource Guide** included at the back of this book.

## SKC Program Learning Outcomes

By the end of **Basic English Level 1**, students will be able to:

- ✅ Introduce themselves
- ✅ Greet others confidently
- ✅ Understand simple classroom instructions
- ✅ Ask and answer simple questions
- ✅ Read short words
- ✅ Write basic sentences
- ✅ Spell names
- ✅ Say phone numbers
- ✅ Fill in simple forms
- ✅ Recognize everyday vocabulary
