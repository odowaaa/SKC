## 📐 Grammar Review

**1. Be Verb:** I am, you are, he/she/it is, we are, they are.
- Example: I am a student. She is a teacher.

**2. This / That / These / Those**
- Example: This is a pen. Those are chairs.

**3. A / An**
- Example: a book, an apple, an engineer.

**4. Plural Nouns (add -s)**
- Example: one book → two books.

**5. Simple Present Tense**
- Example: I study English. She studies English.

**6. Question Words**
- What, Where, When, How, How much, How many, Who, Why.

**7. There is / There are**
- Example: There is a bed. There are two chairs.

**8. Prepositions of Place**
- Example: near, next to, between, in front of, behind.

**9. "By" + Transport**
- Example: I go to school by bus.

**✅ Grammar Practice — Mixed Review**

1. My name __________ Amina. (is/are)
2. __________ is a book. (This/These)
3. She is __________ engineer. (a/an)
4. I have three __________. (book/books)
5. He __________ (study) English every day.
6. __________ is your name? (What/Where)
7. __________ is a bed in my room. (There is/There are)
8. The clinic is __________ the school. (next to/between)
9. I go to school __________ bus. (by/on)

## 👂 Listening Review

**Teacher reads aloud twice.**

*Listening Script:*

> "Hello, my name is Zainab. I am 26 years old, and I am from Kismayo. I have a big family — four brothers and two sisters. My favorite color is purple, and my favorite food is rice with fish. I study at SKC on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. My class starts at nine o'clock. I live in a small house with three rooms. I want to be a teacher. Today, the weather is sunny and warm."

**Listening Questions:**
1. What is her name and age?
2. Where is she from?
3. How many brothers and sisters does she have?
4. What is her favorite color and food?
5. What days does she study?
6. What job does she want?
7. How is the weather today?

## 📚 Reading Review

**Read the passage and answer the questions.**

> My name is Omar. I am a student at SKC. I live in Mogadishu with my family: my parents, two brothers, and one sister. Our house has four rooms. Every morning, I wake up at six o'clock, eat breakfast, and take the bus to school. My English class starts at eight o'clock. I like learning new words every day. My favorite subject is English, and I want to become a teacher in the future. On weekends, I help my mother at the market, buying fruit and vegetables.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. Where does Omar live, and with whom?
2. How many rooms does his house have?
3. What time does he wake up?
4. How does he travel to school?
5. What does he want to become?
6. What does he do on weekends?
