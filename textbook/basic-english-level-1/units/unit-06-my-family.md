### 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will be able to:
- Name family members
- Talk about the size of your family
- Use possessive words (my, your, his, her)
- Describe your family in simple sentences
- Ask about someone else's family

## 💬 Conversation

**Talking About Family**

> **Amina:** Do you have a big family?
> **Faisal:** Yes, I have a big family. I have three brothers and two sisters.
> **Amina:** What about your parents?
> **Faisal:** My father is a teacher, and my mother is a nurse.
> **Amina:** Do you have children?
> **Faisal:** Yes, I have one son and one daughter.
> **Amina:** That's a beautiful family!
> **Faisal:** Thank you! What about your family?
> **Amina:** I have a small family. I have one brother and no sisters.

[Illustration: Two adult students, Amina and Faisal, sitting together at a classroom table, Faisal showing Amina a family photo on his phone, both smiling warmly.]

**👥 Pair Work:** Practice this conversation. Then talk about your own family.

## 📐 Grammar Point: Possessive Words (My, Your, His, Her)

We use possessive words to show who something belongs to:

| Possessive | Example |
|---|---|
| my | This is my mother. |
| your | Is this your sister? |
| his | His father is a driver. |
| her | Her mother is a teacher. |
| our | Our family is big. |
| their | Their house is near school. |

**✅ Practice:** Complete the sentences with the correct possessive word.

1. This is __________ (my/his) mother. (about yourself)
2. Ali has a sister. __________ (Her/His) sister is a doctor.
3. Amina has a brother. __________ (Her/His) brother is a student.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** Distinguishing "his" /hɪz/ and "her" /hɜːr/.

**Practice Drill:** Teacher points to a man → "his mother." Teacher points to a woman → "her father."

"This is his book." "This is her book."

**Tip:** "His" is for males; "her" is for females. Practice with real classmates: "This is his bag." "This is her pen."

## 👂 Listening

**Teacher reads aloud twice.**

*Listening Script:*

> "My name is Khadija. I have a big family. I have four brothers and one sister. My father is a farmer. My mother is a teacher. I have two children, a son and a daughter."

**Listening Questions:**
1. How many brothers does Khadija have?
2. What is her father's job?
3. What is her mother's job?
4. How many children does she have?

---

## 📚 Reading

**Read the short text.**

> This is my family. My father's name is Yusuf. My mother's name is Zainab. I have two brothers and one sister. My grandmother lives with us. We eat dinner together every evening. I love my family very much.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What is the father's name?
2. What is the mother's name?
3. How many brothers and sisters does the writer have?
4. Who lives with the family?
