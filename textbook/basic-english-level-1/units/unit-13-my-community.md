# UNIT 13: MY COMMUNITY

[Illustration: A lively neighborhood street scene showing a mosque, a small market, a clinic, a school, and houses close together, with people greeting neighbors and children playing safely nearby. Flat vector illustration, warm community atmosphere, teal and gold accents.]

### 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will be able to:
- Name places and people in a community
- Ask for and give simple directions
- Talk about your neighborhood
- Use prepositions of place (near, next to, between)
- Describe community helpers

## 💬 Conversation

**Asking for Directions**

> **Amina:** Excuse me, where is the market?
> **Neighbor:** The market is near the mosque.
> **Amina:** Thank you! Is the clinic near here too?
> **Neighbor:** Yes, the clinic is next to the school.
> **Amina:** Thank you very much for your help.
> **Neighbor:** You're welcome!

[Illustration: An adult woman asking a friendly neighbor for directions on a community street, the neighbor pointing toward a nearby mosque and market.]

**👥 Pair Work:** Practice this conversation using different community places.

---

## 📐 Grammar Point: Prepositions of Place

We use these words to describe location:

| Preposition | Example |
|---|---|
| near | The market is near my house. |
| next to | The clinic is next to the school. |
| between | The bank is between the shop and the mosque. |
| in front of | The park is in front of the school. |
| behind | The farm is behind the village. |

**✅ Practice:** Complete the sentences using the correct preposition.

1. The mosque is __________ (near/behind) my house.
2. The clinic is __________ (between/next to) the school.
3. The bank is __________ (in front of/next to) the shop and the market.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** Clear pronunciation of "th" in "the" before place names.

**Practice Drill:** Teacher says → Students repeat:
"The market is near the mosque."
"The clinic is next to the school."

**Tip:** Practice saying "next to" smoothly as one phrase, not two separate words.

## 👂 Listening

**Teacher reads aloud twice.**

*Listening Script:*

> "My community has a market, a mosque, and a school. The market is near the mosque. The school is next to the clinic. I know all my neighbors, and we help each other."

**Listening Questions:**
1. What three places are mentioned?
2. Where is the market located?
3. Where is the school located?

---

## 📚 Reading

**Read the short text.**

> I live in a small community. My neighbors are kind and friendly. There is a mosque near my house, and a market next to the mosque. My children play in the park with other children. When someone is sick, we go to the clinic together. We help each other every day.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. How does the writer describe their neighbors?
2. What is near the writer's house?
3. Where do the children play?
4. What do the community members do for each other?
