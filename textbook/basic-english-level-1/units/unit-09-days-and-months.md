# UNIT 9: DAYS AND MONTHS

[Illustration: A large wall calendar in an SKC classroom, showing a full month grid with Friday highlighted (the weekly holiday in Somalia), a student circling a date with a marker. Flat vector illustration, teal and gold classroom colors.]

### 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will be able to:
- Name the seven days of the week
- Name the twelve months of the year
- Say today's date
- Ask "What day is it?" and "What is the date?"
- Talk about weekly schedules and birthdays

---

## 💬 Conversation

**Talking About the Week**

> **Amina:** What day is it today?
> **Faisal:** Today is Wednesday.
> **Amina:** What do you do on Fridays?
> **Faisal:** On Friday, I go to the mosque and rest with my family.
> **Amina:** What is your favorite month?
> **Faisal:** My favorite month is August. My birthday is in August.
> **Amina:** Happy early birthday!
> **Faisal:** Thank you!

[Illustration: Two students chatting near a classroom calendar, one pointing at a date in August, both smiling.]

**👥 Pair Work:** Practice this conversation. Talk about your own weekly schedule and birthday month.

## 📐 Grammar Point: Prepositions "on" and "in"

We use **"on"** with days, and **"in"** with months and years:

| Preposition | Example |
|---|---|
| on + day | I study on Monday. |
| in + month | My birthday is in July. |
| in + year | I was born in 2000. |

**✅ Practice:** Fill in "on" or "in."

1. I go to school __________ Monday.
2. My birthday is __________ March.
3. We rest __________ Saturday.
4. School starts __________ September.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** Stress patterns in day and month names: "MONday," "TUESday," "JANuary," "FEBruary."

**Practice Drill:** Teacher says → Students repeat, clapping on the stressed syllable.
"MONday, TUESday, WEDnesday, THURSday, FRIday, SATurday, SUNday."

**Tip:** Practice saying the days in order quickly, like a chant, to build fluency.

## 👂 Listening

**Teacher reads aloud twice.**

*Listening Script:*

> "My English class is on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. My birthday is in June. Today is Tuesday, and tomorrow is Wednesday."

**Listening Questions:**
1. Which days does the class meet?
2. Which month is the speaker's birthday?
3. What day is tomorrow?

---

## 📚 Reading

**Read the short text.**

> There are seven days in a week and twelve months in a year. My favorite day is Friday because it is a holiday. My favorite month is December because it is cool weather. My birthday is in December too. I always celebrate with my family.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. How many days are in a week?
2. How many months are in a year?
3. What is the writer's favorite day?
4. When is the writer's birthday?
