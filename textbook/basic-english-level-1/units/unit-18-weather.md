## 💬 Conversation

**Talking About the Weather**

> **Amina:** How is the weather today?
> **Faisal:** It is very hot and sunny.
> **Amina:** Yes, I know. Should I bring an umbrella tomorrow?
> **Faisal:** No, I don't think it will rain. It is the dry season now.
> **Amina:** That's good. I will wear light clothes then.
> **Faisal:** Good idea. Stay cool!

[Illustration: Two students talking outside under a bright sun, one fanning themselves, both wearing light, breathable clothing suitable for hot weather.]

**👥 Pair Work:** Practice this conversation. Talk about today's real weather.

---

## 📐 Grammar Point: "It is" + Weather Adjective

We use **"It is"** to describe the weather:

- It is **hot**.
- It is **sunny**.
- It is **raining**. *(action happening now)*
- It is **windy**.

**✅ Practice:** Complete the sentences.

1. It __________ (is/are) very hot today.
2. It __________ raining outside. (is/are)
3. It __________ windy this afternoon. (is/are)

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** The "-y" ending in weather adjectives: "sunny," "rainy," "windy," "cloudy."

**Practice Drill:** Teacher says → Students repeat:
"It is sunny." "It is rainy." "It is windy." "It is cloudy."

**Tip:** Notice how most weather adjectives end in the same "-y" sound — this makes them easier to remember together.

## 👂 Listening

**Teacher reads aloud twice.**

*Listening Script:*

> "Today is hot and sunny. Tomorrow, it might be windy. Next week is the rainy season, so we should prepare our umbrellas."

**Listening Questions:**
1. What is today's weather?
2. What might tomorrow's weather be?
3. What season is coming next week?

---

## 📚 Reading

**Read the short text.**

> Somalia has two main seasons: the dry season and the rainy season. In the dry season, it is very hot and sunny. People wear light clothes and drink a lot of water. In the rainy season, it rains often, and the fields become green. Farmers are happy because their crops can grow.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. How many main seasons does Somalia have?
2. What is the weather like in the dry season?
3. What happens to the fields in the rainy season?
4. Why are farmers happy in the rainy season?

[Illustration: A split illustration showing the same farm landscape in two seasons — dry and brown on one side, green and lush with rain on the other, a farmer standing between them looking pleased at the rainy side.]
