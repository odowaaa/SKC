By the end of this unit, you will be able to:
- Say your full name, age, and country
- Ask and answer "What is your name?" and "Where are you from?"
- Spell your name aloud
- Say and understand simple personal information
- Fill in a simple registration form

## 📐 Grammar Point: "I am" + Personal Information

We use **"I am"** to talk about ourselves:

- I am **Amina**. *(name)*
- I am **22 years old**. *(age)*
- I am **from Somalia**. *(country)*
- I am **a student**. *(job)*
- I am **single**. *(status)*

We use question words to ask others:

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| What is your name? | My name is... |
| How old are you? | I am... years old. |
| Where are you from? | I am from... |
| What is your job? | I am a/an... |

**✅ Practice:** Complete the sentences about yourself.

1. My name is __________.
2. I am __________ years old.
3. I am from __________.
4. I live in __________.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** The letter **"a"** in names (Amina, Ali, Faisal) and numbers.

**Spelling Aloud:** Practice saying the alphabet letters clearly when spelling your name.

A - B - C - D - E - F - G ...

**Practice Drill:**
"What is your name?" → "My name is..."
"How old are you?" → "I am... years old."
"Can you spell that?" → "Yes. ... "

**Tip:** When spelling a name, say each letter slowly and clearly. Pause between letters: "A... M... I... N... A."

## 🎲 Games

**Game 1 — Name Tag Circle:** Sit in a circle. Each student says, "My name is ___," then the next student says, "Her/his name is ___, and my name is ___," continuing around the circle.

**Game 2 — Spelling Bee:** Teacher says a classmate's name. Students race to spell it correctly on the board.
