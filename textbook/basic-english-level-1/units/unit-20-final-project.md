## 📐 Grammar Point: Final Review — Talking About Yourself

Use everything you have learned to describe yourself completely:

- **Be verb:** I am Amina. I am 24 years old.
- **Simple present:** I study English. I work as a tailor.
- **There is/are:** There are four people in my family.
- **Prepositions:** My house is near the market.
- **Question words:** What is your name? Where are you from?

**✅ Practice:** Write five sentences about yourself using five different grammar points from this list.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

**Sound Focus:** Speaking clearly and confidently during a presentation.

**Practice Drill:** Practice saying your project title and introduction sentence three times, getting louder and clearer each time.

"My name is _____, and my project is called _____."

**Tip:** Look at your audience, speak slowly, and take a breath between sentences. Confidence matters as much as correct grammar!

## 🗣️ Speaking — The Final Project Presentation

### Final Project Instructions

Your final project is called **"My Life in Somalia."** You will create a poster or booklet that presents information about yourself using English, and then present it to the class.

**Your project must include the following sections:**

1. **My Introduction** — Name, age, country, city, nationality.
2. **My Family** — Names and jobs of family members.
3. **My House** — A drawing or description of your house and its rooms.
4. **My School** — Your school, favorite subject, and daily schedule.
5. **My Community** — Places near your home.
6. **My Future Goal** — The job you want and why.
7. **Weather and Colors** — Today's weather and your favorite color.

**👥 Presentation Guidelines:**
- Speak for at least 2–3 minutes.
- Use complete sentences.
- Show your poster or booklet pictures as you speak.
- Answer two questions from classmates or the teacher after your presentation.

[Illustration: A step-by-step final-project poster template mockup divided into seven labeled sections (Introduction, Family, House, School, Community, Future Goal, Weather/Colors), each with a small icon and blank space for drawings, ready for students to fill in.]

**⭐ Extension Challenge:** Add a section titled "My Advice for New Students" with three tips for future SKC beginners.

## 👂 Listening

**Teacher reads a sample project presentation aloud.**

*Listening Script:*

> "Hello, everyone! My name is Maryan. I am 25 years old, from Beledweyne. I have a big family: my parents, three brothers, and one sister. My house has four rooms, and I share a bedroom with my sister. I study at SKC, and my favorite subject is English. My community has a market and a mosque near my house. My goal is to become a nurse. Today the weather is sunny, and my favorite color is yellow. Thank you for listening!"

**Listening Questions:**
1. What is Maryan's project about?
2. How many family members does she mention?
3. What is her future goal?
4. What is her favorite color?

## 📚 Reading

**Read the sample final project text.**

> My name is Abdirahman. My project is called "My Life in Somalia." I am 22 years old, and I am from Galkayo. I live with my parents and two sisters. Our house has three rooms: a bedroom, a kitchen, and a living room. I study at SKC, and I love learning English. Near my house, there is a school and a small shop. My goal is to become an engineer. I am proud of everything I have learned this year.

**Comprehension Questions:**
1. What is the title of Abdirahman's project?
2. Where is he from?
3. Describe his house.
4. What is near his house?
5. What is his goal?
