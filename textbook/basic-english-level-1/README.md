## About This Book

- **Level:** CEFR Pre-A1 / Basic English Level 1 of the SKC Survival English Series
- **Audience:** Adult Somali learners, college beginners, zero English background
- **Units:** 20 complete units, each with 17 sections (objectives, warm-up, vocabulary, illustrated words, conversation, grammar, pronunciation, speaking, listening, reading, writing, practice, games, review, homework, vocabulary list, mini assessment)
- **Vocabulary:** Extensive beginner vocabulary across all 20 units, every word paired with a detailed AI-image-generation illustration prompt (see `[Illustration: ...]` placeholders throughout)
- **Assessments:** Mini assessment per unit, plus a full Midterm Exam (after Unit 10) and Final Exam (after Unit 20), each covering Listening, Reading, Grammar, Writing, and Speaking
- **Originality:** Entirely original content written for SKC; not copied or adapted from Cambridge, Oxford, Longman, Macmillan, or any other publisher

## Illustration Placeholders

Every `[Illustration: ...]` bracketed placeholder throughout this book is a ready-to-use prompt for AI image generation tools, written in enough visual detail (subjects, setting, color palette, style) to produce a consistent, culturally appropriate, flat-vector illustration matching the rest of the book.
