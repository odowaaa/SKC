# SKC TEACHER'S RESOURCE GUIDE

## Section 2: Answer Key

[Illustration: A teacher's desk with a red pen, a stack of graded exam papers marked with checkmarks, and a coffee cup, symbolizing grading, warm desk-lamp lighting, flat-vector illustration.]

This answer key covers the objective exercises (Matching, Circle the Correct Word, Fill in the Blank, Unscramble) in the **✅ Practice** section of every unit, plus the Midterm and Final Exams. Listening, Reading comprehension, Speaking, and open-ended Writing tasks should be checked against the unit's script/passage and assessed using teacher judgment, since student wording will vary naturally.
