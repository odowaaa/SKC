# PICTURE DICTIONARY

[Illustration: A colorful open picture-dictionary spread with small labeled illustrations arranged like stamps in a grid — a hand, a house, a bus, a sun — teal and gold decorative border.]

This Picture Dictionary is a quick-reference guide. It groups the most important words from **Basic English Level 1** into topic categories, each with an illustration prompt, so you can review key vocabulary at a glance.

---
