Our Faculties & Schools

SomaliKing College is organized into five schools, each offering certificate and diploma pathways built for real Somali work environments.

English & Communication Information Technology Engineering Social & Human Services Health Skills

School of English and Communication

The foundation program of SKC, taking learners from zero or near-zero ability to strong academic and professional communication.

8 Levels / Modular Certificate to Advanced 6–10 Weeks per Level
View programs →

Level 1 – Survival English

Greetings, personal information, classroom language.

Level 2 – Foundation English

Simple present tense, family, home, daily routines.

Level 3 – Elementary English

Past/future tense, shopping, transport, simple emails.

Level 4 – Pre-Intermediate English

Comparisons, modal verbs, opinion writing.

Level 5 – Intermediate English

Meeting language, office communication, presentations.

Level 6 – Upper-Intermediate English

Passive voice, reported speech, report writing, debate.

Level 7 – Advanced English

Argument writing, critical reading, public speaking.

Level 8 – Academic & Professional English

CV writing, interviews, proposals, formal reports.

School of Information Technology

Prepares learners for office work, digital support, business technology, and entry-level technical roles. At least 70% of learning time is hands-on.

6 Months – 2 Years Certificate / Diploma
View programs →

Core Topics

  • Computer fundamentals & safe lab practice
  • Typing, file management & operating systems
  • Word, Excel, PowerPoint & PDF workflows
  • Internet skills, email & cloud basics
  • Cyber safety & data protection
  • Web foundations: HTML, CSS & publishing
  • Computer maintenance & networking basics

Suggested Pathways

  • Certificate in Computer Applications
  • Certificate in Digital Business Support
  • Diploma in IT Support
  • Advanced Diploma in Applied IT

School of Engineering and Technical Studies

Tailored for Somalia's needs in construction, infrastructure, maintenance, water systems, and solar energy, training learners to support projects safely and professionally.

6 Months – 2 Years Certificate / Diploma
View programs →

Core Topics

  • Technical drawing & basic CAD
  • Construction materials & workmanship
  • Civil works basics & site supervision
  • Electrical principles & basic wiring
  • Solar power systems installation
  • Plumbing, water systems & sanitation
  • Workshop tools, maintenance & safety
  • Capstone project: repair, installation, or small construction design

Suggested Pathways

  • Certificate in General Construction Support
  • Certificate in Electrical and Solar Basics
  • Certificate in Plumbing and Water Systems
  • Diploma in Applied Engineering Support

School of Social and Human Services

Develops the human and social skills needed in offices, NGOs, public service, and community organizations — where communication, trust, service, and leadership matter most.

3–12 Months Certificate / Professional Skills
View programs →

Core Topics

  • Communication & interpersonal skills
  • Leadership, teamwork & conflict resolution
  • Customer service & front desk professionalism
  • Office administration & records management
  • Report writing, minute taking & meeting skills
  • Community mobilization & civic responsibility
  • Entrepreneurship & small business skills
  • Child protection & safeguarding awareness

Graduate Outcomes

  • Support worker in NGOs and community projects
  • Office or front desk assistant
  • Community mobilizer
  • Program support assistant
  • Entrepreneurial & small business starter

School of Health Skills

Focuses on non-clinical, foundational health competencies that support communities, schools, offices, and local services. It does not replace formal medical training.

3–12 Months Certificate / Community Health
View programs →

Core Topics

  • Basic human body systems & health vocabulary
  • First aid & emergency response basics
  • Hygiene, sanitation & infection prevention
  • Community health promotion & awareness
  • Nutrition & healthy living
  • Maternal, child & family health awareness
  • WASH basics for homes, schools & workplaces
  • Referral procedures & when to seek professional help

Suggested Pathways

  • Certificate in Community Health Support
  • Certificate in First Aid and Safety
  • Certificate in WASH and Hygiene Promotion
