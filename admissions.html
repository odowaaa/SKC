Admissions

SKC welcomes learners from every background — school leavers, adults returning to study, working professionals seeking promotion, and learners with limited formal schooling.

Admission Requirements

Entry Rules by Program Level

Program Level Entry Requirement
English Foundation & Short Skills Courses Open admission — no prior qualification required.
Professional Certificate Courses Basic literacy required; placement testing where applicable.
Diploma Programs Completion of the relevant certificate, or equivalent work experience.

Open Admission

Beginner English and short skills courses are open to all learners.

Placement Testing

Used for English, IT, and diploma-level studies so learners start at the right level.

Recognition of Prior Learning

Work experience or prior training may be recognized toward placement.

Bridging Classes

Extra support for learners who need preparation before joining higher programs.

Progression

Competency-Based Progression

A learner moves to the next level only after meeting the learning outcomes, attending enough classes, and completing the required practical work. Progress is not based on time alone — it's based on demonstrated skill.

  • Continuous assessment alongside end-of-level practical exams
  • Learners must pass both theory and practical requirements where applicable
  • Attendance and conduct may be included in the final result
