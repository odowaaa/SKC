About SomaliKing College

A Career-Focused College for Somali Learners

SKC prepares Somali learners for a better future through strong English, professional skills, practical training, discipline, and service to society — using a competency-based approach where learners must demonstrate what they can do, not only what they can remember.

  • Practical, competency-based curriculum built for Somalia's work environments
  • Flexible full-time, evening, weekend, and blended class schedules
  • Certificate and diploma pathways across five specialized Schools
